Media & Education

Open tutorials, workshops, and learning resources for reproducible neuroscience.

Neuroprocessing is building a structured educational track around Python-based neuroscience, reproducible workflows, and neuroengineering tools. Our goal is to make high-quality lab methods accessible, practical, and shareable.

Python Neuroscience Open Learning
Content Strategy Built for long-term training
  • Hands-on tutorials (real datasets, real pipelines).
  • Mini-projects aligned with research-grade workflows.
  • Open notebooks and reproducible code releases.

We publish content gradually as each module becomes stable and validated.
