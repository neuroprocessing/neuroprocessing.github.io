Contact

Research collaboration, grants, and open-science partnership inquiries.

Neuroprocessing is open to collaborations with research labs, postdoctoral teams, and translational partners building reproducible neuroscience and AI systems.

Official Channels Response window: 3-5 days
  • Email: lab@neuroprocessing.org
  • GitHub: github.com/neuroprocessing
  • Funding: grant, sponsorship, and joint project inquiries.

Research Collaboration Inquiries

What to include in your first message.

  • Institution and team details with domain expertise.
  • Research objective and methodological scope.
  • Data and tooling context including formats and constraints.
  • Timeline and funding context for realistic planning.

Future Funding Opportunities

We are interested in grant consortia and mission-aligned sponsorships.

  • Academic grants: interdisciplinary AI and neuroscience calls.
  • Research projects: co-led initiatives with shared outputs.
  • Sponsorships: educational and open-tool development support.
