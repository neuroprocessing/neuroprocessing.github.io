Collaboration Requests

What to include in your first email.

A structured message helps us quickly evaluate feasibility and define an initial plan.

  • Institution / Lab: name and research domain.
  • Dataset type: EEG / imaging / behavior / molecular.
  • Experimental model: rat/mouse strain and disease model (if applicable).
  • Goal: paper, grant, pipeline development, or pilot experiment.
  • Timeline: expected milestones or deadline constraints.
  • Expected output: open-source tool, publication, dataset release, etc.
