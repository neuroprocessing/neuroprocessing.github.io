Workflow Philosophy

From animal experiments to publishable computational evidence.

We treat neuroscience as an engineering discipline: define protocols, acquire high-quality data, validate assumptions, and publish complete reproducible systems.

  • Step 1: Experimental design + standardized protocols.
  • Step 2: Data acquisition (EEG, PET, behavior, molecular assays).
  • Step 3: Reproducible preprocessing + quality control gates.
  • Step 4: AI modeling + interpretability constraints.
  • Step 5: Biological validation and cross-study replication.
  • Step 6: Open release of code, configs, and versioned reports.
