Methodological Approach

From raw neural data to validated, shareable scientific outputs.

Our approach combines reproducible preprocessing, model transparency, and quantitative uncertainty reporting.

  • Step 1: Protocolized data ingestion and QC gates.
  • Step 2: Model training with interpretable baselines.
  • Step 3: Replication tests across datasets and labs.
  • Step 4: Open publication of code, configs, and reports.
