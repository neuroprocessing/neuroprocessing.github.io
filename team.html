Team

A two-person neuroscience team built for full-stack research.

Neuroprocessing is structured as an integrated research unit: neural engineering and computational pipelines combined with behavioral neuroscience and molecular validation. This hybrid design enables end-to-end experimental studies with reproducible outputs.
